About Us

Established in 2019.This is a Family owned buisness. Rob Howard has always had a passion for fishing and dreamed of opening a bait shop. When he saw the old bait shop on croft road sitting empty and the fishing community slowing due to loosing all of their local access to live bait he acted. He secured the building at the old Lakeside General Store and recruited his wife Melinda and Brother Bill to be co-owners. A month later we had the grand opening of Howard's Lakeside General Store. Everyone you see working at the store has shared ownership and are motivated to keep their customers satisfied.Stop in and see us at Howard's Lakeside we have everything you need to get your boat on the water lots of new rods and more!! Open 7 days a week!
